About Monarch Tales

Monarch Tales began from real story creation and is growing into a brand that turns children into the heroes of meaningful keepsake books.

Our mission

At Monarch Tales, we create personalized storybooks that turn every child into the hero of their own adventure. Our mission is to inspire imagination, strengthen family bonds, and leave children with timeless lessons of love, courage, and resilience.

Why it matters

Stories shape identity. When a child sees themselves inside a beautifully made story, it can deepen confidence, belonging, wonder, and emotional connection.

What guides us

Personalization, creativity, connection, inclusion, and legacy are at the heart of Monarch Tales. Each story is crafted to feel meaningful enough to keep, gift, and remember.

Where we’re going

We begin with personalized books in physical and digital form. Over time, the wider vision can grow into audiobooks, animation, and digital story experiences.

How it works

A simple, thoughtful process designed to feel easy for parents and meaningful for children.

1

Begin with a simple request

Start with a short form that helps us understand the basics — your child, the kind of story experience you want, and how you’d like us to guide the process.

2

Share the deeper story details

Next, we gather the emotional and personal details that shape the heart of the book — your child’s personality, meaningful relationships, and the truths you want the story to carry.

3

Receive a keepsake worth remembering

Your Monarch Tale is then shaped into a warm, beautifully crafted experience designed to feel personal, gift-worthy, and meaningful enough to keep.
