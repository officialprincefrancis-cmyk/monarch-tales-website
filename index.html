About Monarch Tales

Monarch Tales began from real story creation and is growing into a brand that turns children into the heroes of meaningful keepsake books.

Our mission

To help families preserve meaningful chapters before they disappear. Childhood changes quickly. Memories fade. Photographs capture what life looked like — stories capture what life felt like. Monarch Tales transforms real memories, personalities, milestones, and family moments into personalized keepsakes designed to be treasured for years to come.

Why it matters

Stories shape identity. When a child sees themselves inside a beautifully made story, it can deepen confidence, belonging, wonder, and emotional connection.

What guides us

Personalization, creativity, connection, inclusion, and legacy are at the heart of Monarch Tales. Each story is crafted to feel meaningful enough to keep, gift, and remember.

Built with care from beginning to end

From the first story details to the final digital or printed book, Monarch Tales is designed to feel guided, thoughtful, and deeply personal.

Meet the Founder

Built by a storyteller.

Monarch Tales was founded by Prince Francix, a storyteller, musician, and creative founder creating personalized keepsake storybooks for families across Australia, the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

Every story is guided with care, imagination, and respect for the child and family behind it.

Frequently asked questions

These are the questions customers are likely to ask before beginning.

How personalized is each book?

Each Monarch Tale is shaped around the child’s name, personality, emotional world, relationships, and story direction. It is not a simple name-swap book.

How are character visuals created?

Customers can share a Google Drive or Dropbox photo folder link for stronger likeness, or describe the child’s look and personality for a description-based build.

How long does delivery take?

Digital stories usually take 5–7 days. Printed books usually take 2–4 weeks depending on package and production stage.

Do you reuse stories?

No. Every story is created from scratch for the child and family who ordered it. We may use a consistent process, but the story itself is not reused.

How does payment work?

Direct package orders are completed securely through Stripe. If you request a free 1-page preview first, we review your preview details and send the next step if you’d like to continue.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Monarch Tales is designed for families around the world. Printed books can be shipped internationally. Delivery timing may vary depending on your location.

Is this made with AI?

Monarch Tales uses modern creative technology to assist parts of the production process, but every story is planned, guided, edited, and shaped with human creative direction. The goal is not to produce a generic AI book — it is to create a thoughtful story around one real child and family.

Can I see a preview before paying?

Yes. You can request a free 1-page preview before choosing a package. You are never required to continue after receiving your preview.

What if I don’t have many details?

That’s completely fine. The preview and story forms are designed to guide you with simple questions. If we need clarification, we’ll contact you before production begins.

Can grandparents order?

Yes. Grandparents, aunties, uncles, godparents, and family friends can order a Monarch Tale as a meaningful gift, provided they can share enough details to guide the story.

Can I order as a gift?

Yes. Monarch Tales makes a meaningful birthday, Christmas, milestone, school, or family keepsake gift.

Will my child’s details remain private?

Yes. Child and family details are used only to create your story and are treated with care. We do not share personal story details publicly without permission.

What happens after I submit my form?

If you submit a preview request, we review your details and prepare your complimentary 1-page preview. If you place a package order, you’ll complete the full story form so we can begin creating the complete book.
