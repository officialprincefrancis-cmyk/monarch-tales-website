About Monarch Tales

Monarch Tales began from real story creation and is growing into a brand that turns children into the heroes of meaningful keepsake books.

Our mission

At Monarch Tales, we create personalized storybooks that turn every child into the hero of their own adventure. Our mission is to inspire imagination, strengthen family bonds, and leave children with timeless lessons of love, courage, and resilience.

Why it matters

Stories shape identity. When a child sees themselves inside a beautifully made story, it can deepen confidence, belonging, wonder, and emotional connection.

What guides us

Personalization, creativity, connection, inclusion, and legacy are at the heart of Monarch Tales. Each story is crafted to feel meaningful enough to keep, gift, and remember.

Where we’re going

We begin with personalized books in physical and digital form. Over time, the wider vision can grow into audiobooks, animations, and digital story experiences.

How character visuals can be created

We can shape the child’s character in two simple ways, depending on what the family is comfortable with.

Story-based direction

Characters can be illustrated from the child’s personality, story world, favorite colors, and identity details.

Photo-based direction

Customers can share a child photo for stronger likeness when creating visual references and illustrated identity.

Flexible for launch

At launch, the easiest system is to allow either a reference photo or a detailed written description so the process stays flexible and easy.

Frequently asked questions

These are the questions customers are likely to ask before beginning.

How personalized is each book?

Each Monarch Tale is shaped around the child’s name, identity, personality, and emotional details so the final story feels meaningfully connected to them.

How are character visuals created?

Customers can either provide a child photo for stronger likeness or describe the child’s look and personality in detail for a description-based build.

How long does delivery take?

Digital stories usually take 5–7 days. Printed books usually take 2–4 weeks depending on package and production stage.

Is each story pre-made?

No. Each story is carefully crafted after your order and shaped around the child’s identity, personality, and world.

How does payment work?

After your request is reviewed, you’ll receive a secure Stripe payment link to begin your story.
