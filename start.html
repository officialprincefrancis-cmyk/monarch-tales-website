This is the first step in creating a personalized Monarch Tales book. We begin with a simple order stage, then move into the deeper story details that make the final book feel truly personal.

What this step is for

This page helps you begin the process without overwhelm. You do not need to answer every deep story question yet. First, we gather the essential details about your child and the kind of book experience you want.

What happens next

The Monarch Tales process is simple, thoughtful, and guided from beginning to end.

1

Start the request

Fill in the short order form with the first essential details about your child and your preferred format.

2

Share the deeper story

After that, we gather the emotional and personal story details that make the book feel connected and meaningful.

3

Receive a keepsake

Your child’s Monarch Tale is created as a warm, beautifully crafted experience designed to be kept and remembered.

Why Monarch Tales takes this approach

A meaningful personalized story should feel intentional, not rushed. That is why Monarch Tales begins with a clear first step and builds carefully into the full story creation process.

We want families to feel guided, not overwhelmed — and we want each child’s book to carry real warmth, identity, and emotional value.
